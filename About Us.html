About Our Team

We are the Crusader Creators OLGC Robotics team. Our Strategy this year is to allow our robot to use an IMU (Inertial Mass Unit) to help our robot position itself correctly when turning. We would also like to use vision sensing software to help our robot sense objects in front of it. Vision software will help our robot know when to turn or move if it sees certain objects. Our team is going to learn more javascript by taking javascript classes so we can better understand blockly coding. Unlike last year, in the 2020 season we are going to install mechanum wheels on our robot so it can go sideways without turning allowing more free range of movement.
